Profile

Who is Pablo?

Cybersecurity professional moving into cloud security and infrastructure as code. I design and deploy complete environments on AWS with Terraform — bringing together detection (ELK SIEM), identity management (Keycloak) and honeypots to analyse real attacks.

My path started in telecommunications — installing fiber optic networks and configuring systems — which gives me a deep, hardware-level understanding of the infrastructure I now protect. I reoriented toward security deliberately, earning a Master's in Cybersecurity and certifications (eJPTv2, ISC2 CC, Cisco) along the way.

I operate on both sides of the firewall — as a red teamer exploiting web vulnerabilities and as a blue teamer deploying detection, hunting threats and managing identities. Based in Cádiz, Spain, with full geographic mobility and B2 English (C1 in progress).

Honeypot-SIEM on AWS (IaC)

A complete honeypot infrastructure deployed on AWS, defined entirely as code with Terraform. Three honeypots — Cowrie (SSH), Dionaea (malware, via Docker) and a web honeypot — ship logs through Filebeat into a centralized ELK SIEM, with Logstash GeoIP enrichment and Kibana dashboards mapping attacks and captured credentials in real time. Hardened: IP-restricted admin SSH, private Kibana, VPC-locked Elasticsearch.
