Profile

Who is Pablo?

Junior cybersecurity professional with a solid foundation in penetration testing, digital forensics, and incident response. My path started in telecommunications — installing fiber optic networks and configuring systems — which gave me a deep, hardware-level understanding of the infrastructure I now protect.

Specialized through a Master's in Cybersecurity (IT Environments), I've applied that knowledge on real challenges: CTF competitions on TryHackMe and HackTheBox, forensic investigations on CyberDefenders, and hands-on lab environments I built myself.

I operate comfortably on both sides of the firewall — as a red teamer exploiting vulnerabilities and as a blue teamer monitoring, hunting threats, and containing incidents. I'm fluent in Spanish (native) and English (B2/C1 in progress), based in El Puerto de Santa María, Cádiz, with full geographic mobility.
